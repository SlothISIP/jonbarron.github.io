Overview

Industrial NeRF Factory is an end-to-end manufacturing AI platform that integrates Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) for synthetic data generation with digital twin simulation capabilities. The 8-phase pipeline enables sim-to-real transfer for industrial applications, achieving 94.2% simulation success rate and 89.7% real-world deployment performance.

Training Pipeline Architecture


Comprehensive visualization of the NeRF training pipeline architecture, illustrating the data flow from raw multi-view images through neural network training to final 3D scene reconstruction. The pipeline incorporates positional encoding, ray marching, and volume rendering stages for photorealistic novel view synthesis.

Side-by-side comparison demonstrating the sim-to-real transfer capabilities. Multi-View Camera Input Grid


Grid visualization of the 36 camera viewpoints used for NeRF training. Each image captures the industrial target from a different angle, providing comprehensive coverage for accurate 3D reconstruction. Systematic multi-view acquisition ensures consistent lighting and calibrated camera poses.

Rendering Quality Comparison


Quality comparison between different rendering methods and resolutions. The visualization demonstrates PSNR, SSIM, and LPIPS metrics across various NeRF configurations, highlighting trade-offs between rendering speed and visual fidelity for industrial inspection applications.
